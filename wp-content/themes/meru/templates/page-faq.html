What activities are available at the resort?

Our resort offers a variety of activities including beach sports, yoga classes, cultural performances, cooking classes, spa treatments, and excursions to nearby attractions. We also have swimming pools, fitness facilities, and kids' clubs to cater to different interests.

How do I make a reservation at the resort?

Reservations can be made online through our website, via email, or by contacting our reservations team directly. You can also book through travel agents or third-party booking platforms.

Are meals included in the room rate?

Meal options vary depending on the room package you choose. Some packages may include breakfast, while others may offer half-board or full-board options. Our resort also has several restaurants and bars where you can enjoy a variety of cuisines.

Is there a shuttle service available from the airport to the resort?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services for our guests. You can arrange for airport pickup and drop-off by contacting our concierge team in advance.

Can the resort help with arranging tours and excursions in Bali?

Yes, our concierge team can assist you in organizing tours and excursions to popular attractions in Bali such as Ubud, Tanah Lot Temple, Uluwatu Temple, and more. We can also arrange for activities like snorkeling, diving, surfing, and trekking.

Is the resort child-friendly?

Yes, our resort is family-friendly and offers a range of amenities and activities for children including kids' clubs, children's pools, babysitting services, and family-friendly dining options.

Are there any spa facilities available at the resort?

Yes, we have a spa and wellness center where guests can indulge in a variety of rejuvenating treatments and massages inspired by traditional Balinese techniques.

What currency is accepted at the resort?

The official currency of Bali is Indonesian Rupiah (IDR), but US dollars and major credit cards are widely accepted at most establishments, including our resort.
