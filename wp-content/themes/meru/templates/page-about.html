History

In Ancient Sanskrit, the term 'Meru' signifies 'Peak' or 'Mountain.' Within Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain cosmologies, 'Meru' is revered as the 'Center of the Universe' and the divine dwelling place of the Gods.

Getting Here

Exit Ngurah Rai Airport: Upon exiting the airport, head northeast on Jalan Airport Ngurah Rai. Take Jalan By Pass Ngurah Rai: After a short distance, you will reach a roundabout. Take the second exit onto Jalan By Pass Ngurah Rai. Continue on Jalan By Pass Ngurah Rai: Stay on Jalan By Pass Ngurah Rai and follow the signs for Sanur. Enter Sanur Area: As you approach the Sanur area, you will pass through several intersections. Continue straight ahead, following the signs towards Sanur. Turn onto Jalan Hang Tuah: Eventually, you will reach an intersection where you'll turn right onto Jalan Hang Tuah. This road leads directly to The Meru Sanur
